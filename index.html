Who I Am

Hello! My name is Halle Hartman, and I’m a UMKC student with a BA in English and a minor in Editing. I have a passion for learning and writing about technology, whether it’s software, hardware, or general concepts that the average user benefits from knowing. I coded this simple website myself to demonstrate that any technical subject can be learned, taught, and embodied by the laypeople.

I’ve spent my life reading and writing, two habits that I believe everyone deserves the opportunity to enjoy. My greatest values are communication, ingenuity, and understanding— all qualities that a technical writer needs to succeed. Please look through my website, and I look forward to writing for your business.
