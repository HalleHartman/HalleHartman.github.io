An Introduction to JavaScript Classes

Here I have a brief introduction to JavaScript classes, which are a fundamental part of object-oriented programming. Classes are essentially templates that allow you to create numerous objects with pre-set values and methods. This guide is intended for learners with a basic knowledge of JavaScript, who are looking to branch out into slightly more advanced concepts.

Tag Conversions and Actions in Mp3Tag

A guide to tag conversion and actions in Mp3Tag, which is software used for tagging and organizing music files. Tags, such as the title, artist, or album, are essential parts of music files’ metadata. Without tagging software like Mp3Tag, changing or updating metadata would be a time-consuming process. This guide introduces the user to some of the basic functions of Mp3Tag, including pictures and examples.

Introduction to Regular Expressions in Mp3Tag

An overview of regular expressions in the context of Mp3Tag. Regular expressions are a sequence of characters that specifies a “match pattern” for text of any length, and have applications across computing. In Mp3Tag, regular expressions are a powerful tool that allows users to find and change information across tag fields. While they may seem complicated or intimidating to beginners, their usefulness makes them worth pursuing. This guide has many examples of regular expressions to demonstrate the syntax.
